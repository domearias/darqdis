01 — Why Revolut

Revolut builds products people trust with their money. Physical space should earn that same trust the moment someone walks in.

I'm an architect and interior designer who has spent the last years making commercial spaces communicate a brand — in retail, hospitality and, as a project manager, across full architectural builds. I care about the details users never name but always feel: how a room is entered, where the eye lands, how a material ages.

That's the craft I want to bring to Revolut's offices and physical presence — spaces that feel as considered, fast and human as the app itself.
